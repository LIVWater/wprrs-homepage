Sponsors & partners.

The organisations whose support keeps over 250 referees on the field every weekend — across senior, junior, and development levels of Western Province rugby.

One of the world leaders in the recruitment, training, and retention of rugby referees.

Seven of the 27 referees on South Africa's national panel started here. Six have officiated international Test matches. The WPRRS is a proven pathway, and sponsor support — financial or product — is what funds the development that keeps producing world-class match officials.

How partnership works

Where the support lands.

Every rand of sponsor money is allocated to a defined development programme — not general overhead. Partners receive an annual report tracing impact directly to outcomes.

01
Junior recruitment

The Beginners Course intake — application fees subsidised, kit issued to new juniors, transport stipends for development matches.

02
Regional coaching

Weekly coaching sessions in every region — venue hire, coach honoraria, equipment, and travel support.

03
Certification courses

World Rugby Level 1 and Level 2 in-person courses delivered locally — facilitators, materials, and assessment support.

04
Fitness & welfare

Annual YOYO fitness testing, BokSmart re-accreditation, and physio support for referees managing injuries.

05
WHISTLE platform

Hosting, development, and operations of the digital platform that runs Society appointments, reports, and member admin.

06
Awards & recognition

The annual prestige awards evening — venue, hospitality, and the awards themselves — recognising the season's outstanding members.

Become a partner

Two ways to help.

Financial support and product support are both welcomed. Sponsorship can be at Society-wide level or targeted at a specific development programme.

01

Choose your scope

Society-wide partnership for category-exclusive visibility across every fixture, training session, and member event — or targeted partnership against a specific development programme (junior recruitment, BokSmart, awards).

02

Get in touch

Send a brief note via the contact form, or call the office. We'll arrange a short meeting with the Marketing & Recruitment portfolio holder to walk through scope, deliverables, and pricing.

03

Agreement & activation

A simple partnership memorandum defines what you provide, what we deliver in visibility and member access, and how impact is reported. Activation typically starts within four weeks of signature.

04

Annual reporting

Partners receive a dedicated section of the Society's Annual Report — programme-specific outcomes, member feedback, and the year's recognised milestones tied to your support.
