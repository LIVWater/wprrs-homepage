Western Province Rugby Referees' Society — Est. 1992

An affiliate of the Western Province Rugby Union — managing the recruitment, training, grading and appointment of referees for sanctioned games across the province.

With over 250 active referees and 33 years of service, the Society supports the game from grassroots community clubs to provincial competitions — ensuring every match is officiated by a trained, graded, and committed official who upholds the values of rugby.

Our values

The principles that guide every decision on and off the field.

01
Value of six

Accountability

We own our decisions and take responsibility for outcomes at every level of the game. The whistle does not pass blame upwards.

02
Value of six

Trustworthiness

Players, coaches, and unions trust us to be consistent, fair, and above reproach. Trust is built one fixture at a time, lost in one moment.

03
Value of six

Mutual Respect

We treat every player, coach, and official with the dignity the game demands, regardless of grade or competition.

04
Value of six

Truth-telling

Integrity in how we communicate — on the field, in the report, and within the Society — is non-negotiable.

05
Value of six

Professionalism

Every referee represents the Society. We hold ourselves to world-class standards in conduct and preparation, on every appointment.

06
Value of six

Total Commitment

Dedication to the craft, to the game, and to the ongoing development of referees at every grade, every weekend of the season.

WHISTLE Platform

This Saturday.

Every appointment, every venue, every protocol — in one tap. Open WHISTLE on a Friday and your whole weekend is laid out.

  • Kick-off, ground, and grade for each fixture
  • Decline or swap with a tap, routed to the appointments officer
  • Push reminders 24 hours before kick-off

Reports in a minute.

Pre-populated forms for every fixture. Score, cards, and incidents file straight to the Society — and route to discipline automatically when needed.

  • Half-time and full-time scores, side-by-side
  • Cards, citings and HIAs structured for the disciplinary process
  • Drafts saved on-device until you have signal again

Your grade, live.

Fitness, exam, on-field merit — updated after every appointment. Your Sunday wrap-up arrives before you wake up.

  • YOYO fitness scores tracked against your division
  • On-field grading from reviewers in your inbox
  • Sunday wrap: fixtures officiated, cards, reviews returned

20 July 2025

Incident Report: Physical Assault of a Match Official

The WPRRS reports a serious physical assault involving one of our referees during a Manenberg Rangers vs. Albions fixture. The Society condemns all forms of violence against match officials in the strongest terms and is engaging with relevant authorities.

Read full report →
7 March 2025

World Rugby Introduces Experimental Law Changes

Under World Rugby's community rugby review, experimental law changes have been introduced at grassroots level. All active referees must review the updated protocols ahead of the upcoming season. A summary briefing has been distributed to all registered members.
