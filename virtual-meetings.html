Members online, fortnightly.

A standing fortnightly forum for active members — law clarifications, season briefings, guest speakers, and member Q&A. Open to all active members across the Province.

One place, every other Tuesday, where the whole Society talks to itself.

Virtual meetings were introduced during the pandemic and have stuck because they work — members in every region can join from home after work, sessions are recorded, and the agenda mixes law content, season planning, guest perspectives, and open member Q&A. Every active member is welcome.

How to join

Calendar, link, join.

Joining is straightforward — the meeting link rotates with each session and lands in your inbox the day before.

01

Diarise the schedule

Every second Tuesday at 19:30 SAST. The standing schedule runs throughout the playing season, with a reduced cadence over December.

02

Watch for the link

The join link is sent by email to all registered active members on the Monday before each meeting. Check your spam folder if it doesn't land — the sender is administration@wprugby.co.za.

03

Join via Microsoft Teams

Sessions run on Microsoft Teams. Join via the link in the invitation — no account or download required. Camera optional, mic on mute by default.

04

If you missed it

Every session is recorded and posted to the archive within 48 hours. Members access the archive through WHISTLE, and selected public tributes are available below.
