Incident Report: Physical Assault of a Match Official.

The WPRRS reports a serious physical assault involving one of our referees during a Manenberg Rangers vs. Albions fixture. The Society condemns all forms of violence against match officials in the strongest terms and is engaging with the relevant authorities.
