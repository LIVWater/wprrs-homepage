Prestige awards.

The Willem Brits Award, Referee of the Year, and the full senior and junior rolls — recognising the season's outstanding referees and the standard they set.

Award categories

What we award.

Awards are decided at the executive's annual review and presented at the awards evening in November.

01
Willem Brits Award

The Society's flagship award, named for one of the founding figures of the unified WPRRS.

02
Executive Award

For exceptional service to the Society, on or off the field, over the year.

03
Chairman's Award

A discretionary award by the chair for notable contribution that falls outside other categories.

04
Referee of the Year

The season's stand-out senior or junior performer on the field.

05
Overall Merit

For the highest combined score across fitness, exam, and on-field reviews.

06
Most Improved

For the largest gain across the three grading inputs over the season.

07
Most Promising

For a junior or new senior member whose trajectory points to the higher panels.

08
Highest Examination Mark

For the top score in the annual law and protocol examination.

09
Junior Ambassador

For the junior member who best represents the Society at school and junior fixtures.
