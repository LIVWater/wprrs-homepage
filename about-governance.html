How the Society is run.

Eight portfolios, an elected executive, and three decades of chairs who have shaped the organisation — a structured, accountable governance model.

Portfolios

Eight areas of responsibility.

Operational responsibility for the Society is divided across eight portfolios. Each portfolio holder sits on the executive committee and reports to members at the AGM.

02Coaching of Match Officials

Annual General Meeting

Members elect the executive at the AGM each year. The portfolio holders report on the previous season and present the budget and plan for the year ahead.
