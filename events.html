Society
Standing Schedules

Recurring through the season.

Beyond the calendar above, several activities happen on a recurring basis. Regional coaches confirm exact times and venues with their members each week.

Weekly regional sessions

Monday & Wednesday evenings across most regions during the playing season. Coaches confirm times and venues with their members directly.

Virtual meetings

Every second Tuesday — 19:30 via Microsoft Teams. Open to all active members. Recordings circulated after each session.

Match assessor briefings

Monthly for senior assessors and Division 1 referees. Focus on emerging law interpretations and refereeing protocols.

Junior development squads

Saturdays at selected venues. Targeted programme for promising junior referees aged 14–18.
