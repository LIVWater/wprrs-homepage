Annual reports.

The Society publishes a full annual report each year — chair, treasurer, training, and portfolio updates — presented to members at the AGM.

Each annual report is structured around a standard set of sections — the chair's review of the year, the treasurer's financial report, training and development outcomes, match-day statistics, and an update from each portfolio holder. Reports are made available to all members and published here for the wider rugby community.

Standard sections

What's inside.

Each report follows the same structure so that members can compare year-over-year performance and continuity.

01

Chair's review

An overview of the year — Society highlights, milestones, challenges, and direction set by the executive.

02

Treasurer's report

Audited financial statements, budget vs actual, balance sheet, and the budget proposal for the year ahead.

03

Training & development

Courses delivered, certifications issued, fitness-test results, and outcomes from the year's regional coaching programme.

04

Match-day statistics

Total appointments, fixtures officiated by grade, no-shows, and a breakdown of competition coverage.

05

Portfolio updates

A one-page update from each portfolio holder — Appointments, Coaching, Reviewing, Pathway, Marketing, Membership, Education, and Finance.

06

Awards & recognitions

The year's prestige award winners, honorary members appointed, and recognition of long-service members.
