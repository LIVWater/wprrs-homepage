Sizing Built to play, not pose.

Every item is produced in junior, senior, and women's cuts. Size guides per product live on the order page; the office can advise on bulk orders for clubs.

Shipping Cape Town & courier.

Local collection at the Referees' Office, Newlands. Province-wide courier within 3 working days. National courier on request.

Returns 14-day exchange.

Unworn items can be exchanged for a different size within 14 days of receipt. Custom-embroidered items are non-returnable.
