Got questions?

WhatsApp is fast but it loses context. Email leaves you searching threads. WHISTLE is one place: today's fixtures, your reports, your grading, the Society's communications. Nothing scrolls off. Nothing gets missed.
Appointments are made by the Appointments portfolio every Wednesday for the coming weekend. They land in your WHISTLE inbox with kick-off, venue, competition, both clubs, and any specific protocol. One tap to accept. Notifications by SMS as well, in case you do not open the app.
Score, cards (number, time, offence), injuries reported, and any notes the Society needs to see. The form is short by design. Incident reports route to the disciplinary process automatically and remain confidential to the executive and the Union.
Fitness test results land within 48 hours of the YOYO. Exam marks land within a week of the annual exam. On-field reviews update after each assessor reads your match. You can see the rolling average for the season and your position against the divisional threshold.
Every Sunday at 18:00 SAST, WHISTLE shows you the weekend at a glance: fixtures officiated, reports filed, cards issued, injuries reported. Anything unfinished moves into next week automatically. It is a quiet close to the weekend, not a dashboard.
Tap "Forgot password" on the sign-in screen. If automated reset does not reach you, the administration team can reset within one working day. Contact admin at administration@wprugby.co.za.
Yes. Your data is owned by the Society, hosted in South Africa, POPIA-aligned by default. The Society does not sell or share member data. Match-day data feeds the Society's appointments and disciplinary processes and is otherwise kept confidential to members and the executive.
WHISTLE is included in active membership. No additional subscription, no per-fixture charge. The platform is funded by the Society's main and subsidiary sponsors and reviewed by the AGM each year.
